What is IEEE?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. With over 400,000 members worldwide, IEEE drives innovation through cutting-edge research, industry standards, and educational initiatives.
